# Content Review Checklist (Maintainers + Reviewers)

## MIRROR (Evidence)
- [ ] Anchor/canon basis is clear (if applicable)
- [ ] Definitions are present for key terms
- [ ] Boundaries are explicitly stated (no overreach)

## WATER (Synthesis)
- [ ] Cross-links are justified (“why it connects” + “what it adds”)
- [ ] Synthesis is coherent and consistent
- [ ] Hard question included when relevant
- [ ] Tensions are labeled, not flattened

## FIRE (Application)
- [ ] Practice step is specific and safe
- [ ] Proof artifact is collectable/auditable
- [ ] Guardrails are explicit (no promises, no coercion)

## Governance & Compliance
- [ ] Attribution is complete
- [ ] AI use disclosure is present when needed
- [ ] Disclosures included where relevant (education-only, not advice)
- [ ] No prohibited content (harassment, illegal guidance, etc.)

## Decision
- [ ] Approve
- [ ] Request changes
- [ ] Reject (include reason)
