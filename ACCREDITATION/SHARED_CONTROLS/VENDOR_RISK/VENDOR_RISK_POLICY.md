# Vendor Risk Policy

**Owner:** Governance Lead  
**Review Cadence:** Annual

## Purpose
Ensure vendors/platforms do not compromise:
- student privacy
- safety
- academic integrity
- service continuity

## Applies to
- LMS providers
- metaverse platforms (e.g., TCG World)
- AI providers
- identity/credential providers

## Minimum requirements
1) Data protection commitments
2) Access control capabilities
3) Logging/audit support
4) Moderation/safety tooling (if minors present)
5) Export/portability for records

## Review steps (before adoption)
- vendor intake questionnaire
- risk rating (low/med/high)
- mitigation plan for high-risk vendors
- annual re-review

## Evidence
Maintain a vendor register and review notes.
