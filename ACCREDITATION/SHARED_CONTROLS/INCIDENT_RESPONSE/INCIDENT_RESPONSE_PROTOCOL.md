# Incident Response Protocol

## Severity levels
- Sev 1: immediate safety risk / child safety risk
- Sev 2: privacy breach / major misconduct
- Sev 3: policy violation / system error

## Required steps
1) Contain (stop harm)
2) Preserve evidence (logs, screenshots, timestamps)
3) Notify appropriate leads
4) Investigate
5) Remediate
6) Post-incident review (policy/system updates)

## Time expectations
- Sev 1: same-day containment + leadership notification
- Sev 2: containment within 48 hours
- Sev 3: triage within 7 days
