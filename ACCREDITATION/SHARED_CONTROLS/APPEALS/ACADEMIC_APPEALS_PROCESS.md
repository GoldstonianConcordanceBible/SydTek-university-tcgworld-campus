# Academic Appeals Process

**Owner:** Academic Director  
**Review Cadence:** Annual

## When appeals apply
- grade disputes
- rubric disputes
- placement disputes
- AI-influenced decisions impacting grades or progression

## Steps
1) **Informal resolution** (within 7 days of grade posting)
   - student/parent contacts instructor with specific rubric items
2) **Formal appeal** (within 14 days)
   - submit written appeal + evidence (work, rubric, communications)
3) **Review panel**
   - Academic Director + instructor (or alternate) + neutral reviewer
4) **Decision**
   - written response with rationale within 14 days
5) **Final escalation**
   - Head of School/Provost final review if procedural error alleged

## AI rule
If AI contributed materially:
- provide explanation
- ensure human review
- document corrective action if needed
