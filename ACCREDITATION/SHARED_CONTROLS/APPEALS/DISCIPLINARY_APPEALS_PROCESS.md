# Disciplinary Appeals Process

**Owner:** Student Services / Safeguarding Lead  
**Review Cadence:** Annual

## Applies to
- suspensions
- removals from spaces
- conduct violations with consequences

## Steps
1) Written notice of action + reason
2) Opportunity to respond (student/parent)
3) Review by neutral reviewer not involved in the incident
4) Final decision with documented rationale

## Safety exception
Immediate removal may occur when safety risk is credible.
Appeal may occur after containment.
