# Code of Conduct

## 1. Respect & dignity
No harassment, bullying, hate, intimidation, or discrimination.

## 2. Academic integrity
No cheating, plagiarism, or undisclosed AI submission.

## 3. Metaverse conduct
No impersonation of staff, no deceptive identity play, no sexual content, no violent threats.

## 4. Enforcement
Warnings → temporary suspension → removal depending on severity.
Appeal pathway exists for disciplinary actions.
