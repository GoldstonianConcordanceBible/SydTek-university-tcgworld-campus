# Child Safety Policy (Online + Metaverse)

**Owner:** Safeguarding Lead  
**Review:** Quarterly

## 1. Principles
- Safety over engagement
- Age-appropriate interactions
- Clear reporting pathways
- Minimal data collection

## 2. Required controls
- Identity labels: AI is always labeled
- Moderation coverage during live sessions
- Restricted zones for minors (no token promotion, no financial solicitation)
- Parental consent workflow where applicable

## 3. Reporting
Students/parents can report:
- harassment
- grooming behavior
- threats
- inappropriate content
Reports trigger the incident response protocol.

## 4. Enforcement
- Immediate removal from spaces when risk is credible
- Investigation + documentation
- Parent notification when relevant
