# Content Moderation Policy (Metaverse + Forums)

## 1. Moderation objectives
- Prevent harm
- Maintain educational environment
- Protect minors

## 2. Content rules
Remove:
- hate/harassment
- sexual content involving minors (zero tolerance)
- coercion/manipulation
- doxxing or personal info disclosure

## 3. Logs
Moderation actions must be logged (date, reason, action, reviewer).
