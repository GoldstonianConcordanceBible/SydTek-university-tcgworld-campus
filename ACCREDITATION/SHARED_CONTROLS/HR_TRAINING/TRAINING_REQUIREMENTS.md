# Training Requirements (Institutional)

**Owner:** Governance Lead  
**Review Cadence:** Annual

## Required Trainings (All Staff)
1. Code of Conduct + Anti-Harassment
2. Privacy & Records Handling
3. Academic Integrity + AI Disclosure Rules
4. Incident Response Protocol

## Required Trainings (If working with minors or minor-access spaces)
5. Child Safety & Reporting
6. Appropriate Digital Communication with Minors
7. Metaverse Safety Rules (if applicable)

## Required Trainings (Role-based)
### Teachers / Instructors
- Rubrics + grading fairness
- Explainable feedback standards
- Appeals readiness

### Metaverse Moderators
- Content moderation policy
- Safety escalation runbook
- Evidence preservation rules

### Registrar / Records Staff
- records retention rules
- access requests workflow
- correction and annotation procedures

## Renewal Cadence
- Safeguarding: annual
- Privacy + AI governance: annual
- Moderation + incident response: every 6 months (recommended)
