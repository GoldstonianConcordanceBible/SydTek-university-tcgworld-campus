# Staff Onboarding (Institutional)

**Owner:** Head of School / Provost  
**Review Cadence:** Annual

## Purpose
Standardize onboarding so every staff member understands:
- safeguarding expectations
- privacy and records handling
- AI governance and integrity
- incident response procedures

## Onboarding Steps (Day 0 → Day 30)
### Day 0–3: Access + Identity
- Assign role and permissions (least privilege)
- Confirm identity and signed agreements
- Provide staff handbook and code of conduct

### Day 3–10: Required Trainings
- Child Safety & Safeguarding (required if minors may be present)
- Privacy & Records (FERPA-style controls + GDPR awareness)
- AI in Education Policy + Academic Integrity
- Metaverse Moderation and Safety (if applicable)

### Day 10–20: Instructional Quality (teaching roles)
- Course shell standards
- Rubric standards
- Feedback standards (explainability + appeals readiness)

### Day 20–30: Verification
- Supervisor check-in
- Training completion audit
- Initial observation / review (teaching roles)
- Access recertification (ensure access matches role)

## Documentation
All onboarding artifacts are stored as evidence:
- signed agreements
- training completion logs
- access approvals
- evaluation notes (as applicable)
