# Background Check Policy (Institutional)

**Owner:** Safeguarding Lead  
**Review Cadence:** Annual

## Purpose
Protect students by ensuring appropriate screening for roles with student access.

## Scope
Applies to:
- teachers/instructors
- moderators
- student services staff
- any staff with direct communication access to minors

## Standard
- Background check is required prior to role activation.
- Access to minors or minor spaces is prohibited until clearance is confirmed.

## Data Handling
Background check results are:
- confidential
- stored with restricted access
- retained only as long as necessary for compliance and risk management

## Exceptions
Any exception requires written approval by:
- Head of School/Provost
- Safeguarding Lead
and must include a documented mitigation plan.

## Evidence
Maintain:
- check completion confirmation
- clearance date
- role access grant date
