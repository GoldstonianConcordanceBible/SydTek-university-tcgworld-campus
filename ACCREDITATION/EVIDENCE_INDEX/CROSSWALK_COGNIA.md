# Crosswalk Template: Cognia-style Standards

> Fill with your final mappings once you select your accreditor.

## Standard Area: Leadership & Governance
- Evidence: Board Charter, Review Cadence, Conflict of Interest
- Artifacts: meeting minutes, annual disclosures, transparency reports

## Standard Area: Learning & Teaching
- Evidence: scope/sequence, course catalog, syllabus, instructional model
- Artifacts: course shells, lesson plans, teacher feedback logs

## Standard Area: Assessment & Improvement
- Evidence: assessment plan, KPI dashboard, improvement plan
- Artifacts: benchmark results, action plans, before/after metrics

## Standard Area: Resource Capacity
- Evidence: finance/sustainability policy, staffing plan, security baseline
- Artifacts: budgets, staffing roster, vendor risk reviews

## Standard Area: Culture of Safety & Wellbeing
- Evidence: safeguarding policies, moderation policies, incident response
- Artifacts: training logs, incident reports (redacted), escalation runbooks
