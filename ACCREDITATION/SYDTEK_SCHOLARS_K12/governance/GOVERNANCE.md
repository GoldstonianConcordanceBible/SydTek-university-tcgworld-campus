# Governance (SydTek Scholars)

## Goal
Maintain a trustworthy, high-quality, parent-operable curriculum system.

## Roles
- Maintainers: approve merges, enforce policy
- Reviewers: Tier 2/3 approvals
- Contributors: propose units, docs, improvements

## Review tiers
- Tier 1 Editorial: clarity, format, grade fit
- Tier 2 Academic: sequencing, objectives, assessment validity
- Tier 3 Policy: copyright, non-affiliation language, child-safety, privacy claims

## Merge rules
- New units: Tier 2 + Tier 3 approval
- Ops/legal changes: Tier 3 approval
