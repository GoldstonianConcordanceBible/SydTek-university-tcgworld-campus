# Copyright & Content Policy

## Policy
This repo must not contain:
- scanned pages, copied worksheets, or substantial excerpts from copyrighted textbooks,
- proprietary teacher guides, tests, answer keys, or protected course assets,
- any content that creates market substitution for copyrighted materials.

## Allowed
- original SydTek-authored lessons, rubrics, and assessments,
- high-level summaries (short, non-substitutive),
- lesson plans that reference a resource **without reproducing it**,
- “inspired-by” structures (pacing, sequence, skill ladders) written in original language.

## Citations
If you reference an external book/curriculum, include:
- title, publisher, edition/year (if known),
- what concept it supports,
- no copying of protected pages.

## Enforcement
PRs that violate policy will be rejected.
Repeated violations may result in contributor bans.
