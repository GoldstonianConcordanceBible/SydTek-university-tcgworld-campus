# Mission Statement (SydTek Scholars)

SydTek Scholars exists to provide a rigorous, Judeo-Christian online homeschool education
that forms wise, capable learners through academic excellence, biblical worldview formation,
and responsible use of technology.

We educate students to:
- pursue truth with humility
- love God and neighbor through disciplined living
- master foundational skills and modern literacies
- develop character, responsibility, and service
