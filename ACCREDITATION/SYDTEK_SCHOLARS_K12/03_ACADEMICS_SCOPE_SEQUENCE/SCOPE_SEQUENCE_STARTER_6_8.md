# Scope & Sequence Starter (Grades 6–8)

## Literacy
- analytical reading, argument writing
- multi-source research with citations
- speaking/presentation

## Math
- ratios/proportions, pre-algebra to algebra foundations
- statistics intro

## Science
- cells and body systems
- chemistry basics
- earth/space systems

## Social Studies
- world history survey
- civics foundations and responsible citizenship

## Bible / Worldview
- worldview comparisons (respectful)
- ethics: justice, mercy, humility
- discernment about media + technology

## Technology Literacy
- digital citizenship
- intro to AI literacy (what it is / what it isn’t)
- prompt hygiene + disclosure rules
