# E) Practice Set 4 — Rate of Change in Context (Compute Slope + Units)

1) A car goes from 0 miles to 150 miles in 3 hours. Rate?  
2) A tank drops from 40 gallons to 28 gallons in 4 hours. Rate?  
3) A score rises from 70 to 88 over 6 weeks. Rate?  
4) Temperature goes from 68°F to 50°F in 3 hours. Rate?  
5) Savings goes from $120 to $180 in 5 weeks. Rate?  
6) A plant grows from 9 cm to 21 cm in 6 days. Rate?  
7) A balance goes from $300 to $240 in 5 weeks. Rate?  
8) A trip goes from 10 miles to 10 miles in 2 hours. Rate? (interpret)  

### Answer Key
1) 50 miles/hour  
2) (28−40)/4 = -3 gallons/hour  
3) (88−70)/6 = 3 points/week  
4) (50−68)/3 = -6 °F/hour  
5) (180−120)/5 = 12 dollars/week  
6) (21−9)/6 = 2 cm/day  
7) (240−300)/5 = -12 dollars/week  
8) 0 miles/hour (no change)
