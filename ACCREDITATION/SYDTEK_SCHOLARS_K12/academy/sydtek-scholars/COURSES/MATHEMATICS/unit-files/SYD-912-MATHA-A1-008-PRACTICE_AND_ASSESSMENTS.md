1) Hours studied vs test score  
2) Outside temperature vs hot chocolate sales

### Day 3 (Slope Meaning in Models)
1) If a best-fit line is y = 2x + 10, what does slope 2 mean?  
2) What does intercept 10 mean?  
3) Predict y when x=5.  
4) If actual y was 18 at x=5, is the residual positive or negative?

### Day 4 (Make Predictions)

1) Predict y when x=2

1) Ice cream sales and drownings

### Day 6 (Residual Sense)

2) If predicted is 20 and actual is 17, residual = ?

4) Residual negative means the model ______ the actual.  
5) What does a residual close to 0 mean?

Line A: y = 2x + 1

3) Predict if correlation will be positive/negative/none.

# D) Practice Set 3 — Predictions and Residuals

Use the model **y = 2x + 1**.

1) Predict y when x=6  
2) Predict y when x=10  
3) If actual at x=6 is 14, residual = actual − predicted  
4) If actual at x=10 is 19, residual = ?  
5) Is residual positive or negative in #3 and #4?

### Answer Key
1) 13  
2) 21  
3) 14 − 13 = 1  
4) 19 − 21 = -2  
5) #3 positive, #4 negative

Use the model **y = -3x + 21**.

6) Predict y when x=4  
7) Predict y when x=7  
8) If actual at x=4 is 10, residual = ?  
9) If actual at x=7 is 1, residual = ?  
10) Which prediction is closer (smaller |residual|), #8 or #9?

### Answer Key
6) 9  
7) 0  
8) 10 − 9 = 1  
9) 1 − 0 = 1  
10) tie (both |1|)
