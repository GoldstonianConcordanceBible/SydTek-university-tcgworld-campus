Find x-intercept and y-intercept.

1) y = 2x + 6  
2) y = -3x + 9  
3) y = x - 4  
4) y = -2x - 8  
5) y = 5  
6) y = -x

4) y-int (0,-8); x-int 0=-2x-8 → x=-4 → (-4,0)  
5) y-int (0,5); x-int none (never hits y=0)  
6) y-int (0,0); x-int (0,0)  
7) y-int (0,0); x-int (0,0)  
8) y-int (0,2); x-int 0=-0.5x+2 → x=4 → (4,0)  
9) y-int (0,-12); x-int 0=3x-12 → x=4 → (4,0)  
10) y-int (0,8); x-int 0=-4x+8 → x=2 → (2,0)

# D) Practice Set 3 — Convert Standard Form to Slope-Intercept

Convert:

1) 3x + y = 9  
2) 2x - 3y = 6  
3) -x + 4y = 8  
4) 5x + 2y = 10  
5) x - y = 7  
6) 4x + 2y = 8  
7) 6x + 3y = 12  
8) 2x + y = 5  
9) 3x - y = 3  
10) x + 2y = 4  

### Answer Key
1) y = -3x + 9  
2) -3y = 6 - 2x → y = (2/3)x - 2  
3) 4y = x + 8 → y = (1/4)x + 2  
4) 2y = -5x + 10 → y = (-5/2)x + 5  
5) -y = 7 - x → y = x - 7  
6) 2y = -4x + 8 → y = -2x + 4  
7) 3y = -6x + 12 → y = -2x + 4  
8) y = -2x + 5  
9) -y = 3 - 3x → y = 3x - 3  
10) 2y = -x + 4 → y = (-1/2)x + 2

# E) Practice Set 4 — Point-Slope and Writing Equations

## Part 1: Use point-slope form then convert to y=mx+b
Write equation for each:

1) m=2 through (1,3)  
2) m=-1 through (0,4)  
3) m=1/2 through (2,-2)  
4) m=3 through (-1,5)  
5) m=-2 through (4,1)

### Answer Key (final y=mx+b)
1) y = 2x + 1  
2) y = -x + 4  
3) y = (1/2)x - 3  
4) y = 3x + 8  
5) y = -2x + 9

## Part 2: Two Points → Equation
6) through (0,1) and (2,5)  
7) through (1,4) and (3,0)  
8) through (-2,3) and (2,3)  
9) through (5,-1) and (5,4)  
10) through (-1,-2) and (3,6)

### Answer Key
6) slope (5-1)/(2-0)=2 → y=2x+1  
7) slope (0-4)/(3-1)=-4/2=-2 → y=-2x+6  
8) horizontal y=3  
9) vertical x=5 (not in y=mx+b form)  
10) slope (6-(-2))/(3-(-1))=8/4=2 → use point (-1,-2): y=2x+0 → y=2x
