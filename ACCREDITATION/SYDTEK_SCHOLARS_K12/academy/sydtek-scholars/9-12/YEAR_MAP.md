# Grades 9–12 Year Map (Full Scope & Sequence)

## Graduation outcomes
A graduate can:
- read complex texts and argue with evidence
- write clearly for academic + real-world settings
- complete math/science pathways with mastery evidence
- understand civics/economics and communicate responsibly
- produce a capstone portfolio (Fire) showing service and skill
