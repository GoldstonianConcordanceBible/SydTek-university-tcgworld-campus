# Records & Transcripts (Parent-Operable)

## What to track weekly
- attendance days
- units completed + mastery level
- writing samples (1–2)
- math checks (1)
- reading log (minutes/pages)
- science/social studies artifact (photo or page)
- service/character note (optional)

## Portfolio minimums per year
- 6 writing samples
- 6 math checks
- 4 science artifacts
- 4 history/civics artifacts
- 1 presentation (video or live)

## Transcript approach (9–12)
Credits are earned when:
- objectives met at Level 3
- required assessments complete
- portfolio artifact saved
- final exam/project passed

Recommended credits:
- English: 4
- Math: 3–4
- Science: 3–4
- History: 3–4
- PE/Health: 1–2
- Electives: 4–6
