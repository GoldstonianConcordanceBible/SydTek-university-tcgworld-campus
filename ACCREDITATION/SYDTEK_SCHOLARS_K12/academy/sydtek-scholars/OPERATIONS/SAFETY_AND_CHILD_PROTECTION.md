# Safety & Child Protection (SydTek Scholars)

## Principle
Learning environments must be safe, respectful, age-appropriate, and parent-governed.

## Boundaries
- No collection of student personal data in repo
- No public posting of student identifying info
- No unsafe “challenges” or dares
- No instructions for harmful activities

## Communication
- Parent/guardian controls platform access
- Clear reporting path for concerns (issues/private channel outside repo)

## Content standards
- Age-appropriate discussions only
- No explicit sexual content
- No graphic violence
- Respectful treatment of all people
