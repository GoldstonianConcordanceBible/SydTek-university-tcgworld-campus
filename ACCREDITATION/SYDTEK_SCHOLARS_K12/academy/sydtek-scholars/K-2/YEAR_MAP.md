# K–2 Year Map (Full Scope & Sequence)

## Daily Rhythm (default)
- Bible/Character (15–20m)
- Phonics/Reading (30–45m)
- Handwriting/Writing (15–25m)
- Math (30–40m)
- Science OR Social Studies (20–30m)
- Read-aloud (10–20m)
