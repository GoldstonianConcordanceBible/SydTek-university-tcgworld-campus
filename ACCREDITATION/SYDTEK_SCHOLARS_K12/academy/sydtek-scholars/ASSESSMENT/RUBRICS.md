# Rubrics (Universal)

## Writing (4-point)
4: clear thesis, structure, evidence, few errors
3: clear main idea, organized, minor errors
2: partially organized, weak support, frequent errors
1: unclear, incomplete, heavy support needed

## Reading Comprehension
4: summarizes + infers + cites evidence
3: summarizes + answers questions accurately
2: answers literal questions only; misses key ideas
1: cannot retell; needs decoding/fluency support

## Math
4: correct + explains reasoning + flexible strategies
3: correct with standard method
2: partial accuracy; inconsistent
1: not yet; foundational gaps

## Project/Presentation
4: polished, accurate, persuasive, audience-ready
3: clear, accurate, understandable
2: incomplete or unclear; needs coaching
1: minimal; not meeting requirements
