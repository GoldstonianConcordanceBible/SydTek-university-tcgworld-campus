# Mastery Scale (SydTek Scholars)

## Level 4: Exceeds
Explains clearly, transfers to new situations, minimal help.

## Level 3: Meets
Accurate and independent on grade-level tasks.

## Level 2: Developing
Partly correct; needs prompts, re-teaching, or extra practice.

## Level 1: Beginning
Not yet; needs guided instruction and foundational rebuilding.
