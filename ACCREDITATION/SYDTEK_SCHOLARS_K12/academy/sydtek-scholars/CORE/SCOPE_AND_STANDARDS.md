# SydTek Scholars – Scope & Standards (Flagship)

## Purpose
SydTek Scholars exists to produce learners who are:
- literate, numerate, and articulate,
- grounded in Judeo-Christian formation,
- capable of navigating modern technology with wisdom,
- ready for college, trades, entrepreneurship, and service.

## Competitive edge (why we win)
1. **Audit-ready structure:** every unit is reproducible and reviewable.
2. **Parent-operable design:** clear pacing, rubrics, and record keeping.
3. **Future-ready progression:** AI/computing literacy introduced developmentally.
4. **Formation spine:** Mirror → Water → Fire used to anchor identity, discipline, and vocation.

## Mirror → Water → Fire mapping (simple)
- **Mirror:** identity, reflection, truth-telling, comprehension
- **Water:** practice, repetition, skill-building, fluency
- **Fire:** application, creativity, service, leadership, capstones

## Grade-band focus
- **K–2:** phonics, handwriting, foundational math, Bible stories, habits
- **3–5:** reading stamina, writing, math fluency, science observation, civics basics
- **6–8:** logic, composition, pre-algebra/algebra readiness, labs, digital citizenship
- **9–12:** rhetoric, math pathways, sciences, history, economics, career capstones

## Output artifacts
- unit plans
- rubrics + mastery scale
- records/transcript templates
- optional portfolio and capstone artifacts
