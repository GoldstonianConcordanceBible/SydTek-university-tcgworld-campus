# Certificate Programs

SydTek certificate pathways provide structured non-degree learning programs.

Each certificate should include:

• pathway title  
• learning objectives  
• playlist hours  
• book requirements  
• writing requirements  
• final assessment or project  

---

## Sample Certificates

### Certificate in Gospel Foundations
Requirements:
• 40 watch hours
• 2 books
• 8 written reflections
• final quiz

### Certificate in Biblical Philosophy
Requirements:
• 60 watch hours
• 3 books
• 10 written reflections
• comparative essay

### Certificate in Canon Studies
Requirements:
• 50 watch hours
• 2 books
• 1 canon mapping project
• final assessment

### Certificate in Theology of the Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine
Requirements:
• 35 watch hours
• 1 doctrine text
• 6 written reflections
• final synthesis essay

---

## Completion Standard

A certificate is awarded only when the student completes all required components and has documented evidence in the registry.
