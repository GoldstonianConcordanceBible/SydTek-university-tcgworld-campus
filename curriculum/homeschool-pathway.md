# Homeschool Pathway

This file outlines a sample homeschool pathway structure for SydTek Scholars.

---

## Level Bands

### Lower Level
Focus:
• short playlist segments
• narration
• drawing
• memorization
• guided discussion

### Middle Level
Focus:
• longer playlist sets
• reading summaries
• writing prompts
• simple quizzes
• project artifacts

### Upper Level
Focus:
• sustained watch-hour tracking
• reading logs
• paragraph responses
• short essays
• presentations
• portfolio review

---

## Example Pathway

### Scripture Foundations Pathway

Requirements:
• 30 watch hours
• 1 foundational text
• 10 narrated or written reflections
• 1 end-of-pathway project

Evidence:
• completion log
• parent verification
• submitted reflections
• project artifact

---

## Parent Verification

The homeschool pathway should include a parent or guardian signoff field for each completed unit.

Recommended fields:
• learner name
• pathway name
• date range
• hours completed
• books completed
• reflections completed
• parent signature or digital verification

---

## Notes

This pathway is a framework for structured implementation and must be adapted to local law and reporting requirements where applicable.
