# School of Health Systems & Supply Chain Innovation

## Purpose
Trains learners to analyze and improve complex systems in healthcare operations, supply chains, and technology-enabled workflows.

## Scope
- systems thinking, process improvement, supply chain fundamentals, healthcare operations
- Credentials: certificates and degree pathways
- Delivery: online/hybrid

## Distinctives
- Case-based methods + process mapping
- Capstone: improvement proposal with measurable KPIs
