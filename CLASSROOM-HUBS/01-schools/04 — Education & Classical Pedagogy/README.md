# School of Education & Classical Pedagogy

## Purpose
Develops educators and curriculum designers with strong foundations in learning science, assessment, and structured curriculum design across K–12 and adult education.

## Scope
- curriculum design, MOOC-supported instruction, evaluation, student support models
- Credentials: certificates through graduate-level study
- Delivery: online/hybrid

## Distinctives
- Curriculum mapping and assessment literacy
- Practical course-building studios
- Capstone: publishable curriculum package + assessment plan
