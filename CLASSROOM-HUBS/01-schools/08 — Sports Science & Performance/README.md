# School of Sports Science & Performance

## Purpose
Educates learners in performance training, coaching methods, measurement, and sports entrepreneurship.

## Scope
- training science, coaching, athlete development, facility operations basics
- Credentials: certificates and degree pathways
- Delivery: hybrid with practicum options

## Distinctives
- Performance measurement literacy
- Coaching practicum + safety standards
- Capstone: athlete development plan + measurable outcomes
