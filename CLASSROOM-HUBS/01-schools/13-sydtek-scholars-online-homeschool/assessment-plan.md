# Assessment Plan — SydTek Scholars

## Assessment Cycle
- Benchmarking: 3 times per academic year (fall / winter / spring)
- Reporting: progress reports each term; annual performance report
- Oversight: Academic Director + Assessment Committee
- Documentation: results and action items stored in `evidence-index.md`

## Measures by Outcome
| Outcome | Direct Measure | Benchmark | Indirect Measure | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| SLO-1 Literacy | writing portfolio rubric | 80% meet/exceed | parent/student survey | 80% satisfaction |
| SLO-2 Math | benchmark assessment | 75% proficiency | course completion | 85% completion |
| SLO-3 Science | lab/project rubric | 80% meet/exceed | engagement metric | 80% on pace |
| SLO-4 Civics | essay/project rubric | 80% meet/exceed | survey | 80% satisfaction |
| SLO-5 Faith/Character | reflection rubric + conduct indicators | 80% meet/exceed | survey | 80% satisfaction |

## Continuous Improvement
1. collect assessment data
2. identify gaps
3. implement adjustments (curriculum, pacing, supports)
4. re-measure
5. document improvements and outcomes
