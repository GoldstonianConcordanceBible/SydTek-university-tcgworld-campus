# Programs — SydTek Scholars (K–12)

## K–12 Program Tracks
| Track | Grades | Delivery | Credentialing | Primary Outcomes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Early Learning | K–2 | Online + parent-guided | Progress reports + portfolio | Reading fluency, numeracy, foundational knowledge |
| Elementary | 3–5 | Online + parent-guided | Progress reports + portfolio | Literacy, math competency, science/social studies foundations |
| Middle School | 6–8 | Online | Course completion records | Analytical writing, algebra readiness, scientific reasoning |
| High School | 9–12 | Online | Transcript + credit tracking | College/career readiness, research writing, math/science competency |

## Additional Options (as offered)
- Honors track (accelerated mastery expectations)
- Remediation track (intervention plans with progress monitoring)
- Dual-enrollment readiness track (college credit pathways where applicable)

## Admission & Placement (Summary)
- Enrollment application + parent orientation
- Placement screening (reading/math diagnostics) for appropriate pacing
- Individual learning plan option (ILP) for customized pacing
