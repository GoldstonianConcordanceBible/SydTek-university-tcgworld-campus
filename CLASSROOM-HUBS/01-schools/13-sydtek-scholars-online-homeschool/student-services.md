# Student Services — SydTek Scholars

## Academic Support
- parent onboarding and pacing guidance
- progress monitoring dashboards (as implemented)
- intervention plans for students behind pace
- optional tutoring and office hours (as offered)

## Records and Transcripts
- term progress reports
- course completion documentation
- high school transcripts and credit tracking (9–12)
- portfolio archive for writing and projects

## Student Safeguarding (Online)
- communication standards for staff and students
- privacy and data protection practices
- clear reporting pathways for concerns

## Special Support Considerations
SydTek Scholars may provide accommodations consistent with program capacity and applicable regulations, documented through an Individual Learning Plan (ILP) process.
