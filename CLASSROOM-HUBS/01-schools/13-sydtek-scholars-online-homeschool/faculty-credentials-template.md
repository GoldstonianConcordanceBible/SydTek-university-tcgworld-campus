# Faculty & Staff Roles — Minimum Qualifications (Template)

## Academic Director
- demonstrated leadership in K–12 education (degree and/or documented experience)
- oversight of curriculum, assessment, and quality assurance

## Lead Teacher / Instructional Coach
- relevant degree and/or documented teaching competence
- ability to evaluate student work with rubrics
- training in online instruction practices

## Curriculum Designer
- experience in curriculum writing and standards alignment
- ability to produce scope/sequence and assessments

## Student Support Coordinator
- experience in student services, advising, parent communication
- recordkeeping and intervention planning skills

Evidence of credentials and roles is linked in `evidence-index.md`.
