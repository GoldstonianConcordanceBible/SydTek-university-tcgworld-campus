# Student Learning Outcomes (K–12)

SydTek Scholars students will demonstrate measurable competency in the following outcomes.

SLO-1 Literacy: Read, write, and communicate clearly at grade-appropriate levels using evidence and structured reasoning.
SLO-2 Mathematics: Apply grade-appropriate math concepts accurately, progressing through algebra and beyond as appropriate.
SLO-3 Scientific Reasoning: Use observation, experimentation, and evidence-based explanation to understand the natural world.
SLO-4 Social Studies & Civics: Demonstrate historical literacy, civic understanding, and ethical reasoning for responsible citizenship.
SLO-5 Faith & Character Formation: Demonstrate Judeo-Christian ethical reasoning, character habits, and respectful discourse across subjects.

## Outcome Measures (Examples)
Direct:
- mastery checks and unit assessments
- writing rubrics and portfolio artifacts
- math benchmarks and cumulative exams
- projects and labs with rubrics

Indirect:
- parent/student surveys
- progression rates (unit completion, course completion)
- retention and graduation indicators
