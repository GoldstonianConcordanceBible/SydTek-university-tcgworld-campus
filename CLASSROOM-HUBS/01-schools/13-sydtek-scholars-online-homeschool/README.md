# SydTek Scholars — Judeo-Christian Private Online Homeschool (Accreditation Candidate)

## Identity and Purpose
SydTek Scholars is a Judeo-Christian private online homeschool program delivering structured K–12 instruction through a comprehensive digital curriculum, teacher support, and measurable learning outcomes. SydTek Scholars is designed to operate as an accreditation candidate program with documented governance, assessment, student services, and continuous improvement.

## Educational Model
- Online private homeschool program (parent-directed with school-provided curriculum, teacher support, grading tools, and records)
- Judeo-Christian worldview integrated across subjects (faith-informed, academically rigorous)
- Standards-aligned academic scope and sequence (with documented crosswalks)
- Modular curriculum delivery: unit-based courses, projects, mastery checks, and portfolio artifacts

## Accreditation Candidate Orientation
SydTek Scholars maintains an accreditor-facing quality assurance system:
- clearly defined student learning outcomes (SLOs)
- course-to-outcome curriculum mapping
- documented assessment plan with benchmarks
- student services and safeguarding policies
- faculty qualification standards and role definitions
- annual review cycle and improvement documentation

## Grade Levels Served
- K–12 (with early learning, elementary, middle, and high school pathways)

## Delivery and Instructional Support
- Asynchronous core lessons + scheduled support options
- Parent support: pacing guides, lesson plans, grading rubrics, and progress monitoring
- Teacher support (as applicable): feedback, evaluation, office hours, and intervention planning

## Distinctives
- Faith-integrated instruction without sacrificing academic depth
- Mastery-based progression options and clear measurable benchmarks
- Evidence-driven academic records: transcripts, portfolios, and assessment reports
- Parent support and student success systems built for consistency and scale

## Quality Assurance
SydTek Scholars follows a closed-loop continuous improvement cycle:
Outcomes → Measures → Data Review → Improvements → Documentation.
