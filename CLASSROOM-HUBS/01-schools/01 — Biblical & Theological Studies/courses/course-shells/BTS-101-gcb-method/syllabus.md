# BTS-101 — The GCB Method: Mirror → Water → Fire Hermeneutics

## Course Description
This course teaches the interpretive method used across SydTek University: Mirror (self-examination and accountability), Water (cleansing clarity through context), and Fire (tested conviction and refined application). Students learn how to interpret Scripture with consistency and document their reasoning.

## Recommended Time
6–10 hours total

## Modules
1. Why interpretation frameworks matter
2. Mirror: accountability, bias-checking, motives
3. Water: context, genre, original audience, cross-references
4. Fire: synthesis, doctrine testing, ethical application
5. Common failures: prooftexting, hype cycles, certainty without evidence
6. Capstone: apply Mirror–Water–Fire to one passage
