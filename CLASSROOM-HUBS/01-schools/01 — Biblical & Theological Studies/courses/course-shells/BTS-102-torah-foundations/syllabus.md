# BTS-102 — Torah Foundations: Covenant, Exodus, Law, Tabernacle

## Course Description
A foundation course in the Torah (Genesis–Deuteronomy) focused on covenant structure, deliverance patterns, law as formation, and the theological meaning of tabernacle worship. Students learn how Torah themes shape the prophets and the New Testament.

## Recommended Time
10–15 hours total

## Modules
1. Covenant in Genesis (promise, seed, land, blessing)
2. Exodus as deliverance pattern
3. Sinai: law, identity, and holiness
4. Tabernacle theology (presence, sacrifice, priesthood)
5. Deuteronomy: remembrance, obedience, warning, hope
6. Torah themes that echo into the Gospels

## Final Requirement
Create a “Torah Theme Map” (1–2 pages):
- 6 major themes
- 2 supporting passages per theme
- 1 sentence showing NT connection (cautious, not forced)
