# Program Learning Outcomes (PLOs) — BA Biblical & Theological Studies

PLO-1: Apply a documented hermeneutical method to interpret biblical texts with explicit reasoning and context control.
PLO-2: Demonstrate biblical literacy across major corpora (Torah, Prophets, Writings, Gospels, Epistles, Revelation).
PLO-3: Evaluate theological claims using evidence, genre awareness, and responsible historical context.
PLO-4: Produce research-informed writing with appropriate citation and academic integrity.
PLO-5: Communicate biblical theology responsibly for teaching and public discourse (audience-appropriate, non-sensational).
