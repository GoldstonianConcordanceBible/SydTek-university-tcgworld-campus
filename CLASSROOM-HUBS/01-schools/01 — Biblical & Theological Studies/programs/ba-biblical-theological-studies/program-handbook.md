# Program Handbook — BA in Biblical & Theological Studies

## Overview
- Credential: Bachelor of Arts (BA)
- Delivery: Online (hybrid-ready)
- Total Credits: 120 (typical BA standard)
- Structure: General Education + Major Core + Major Electives + Capstone

## Program Purpose
Graduates will demonstrate biblical literacy, method-based interpretation, theological reasoning, and communication skills suitable for ministry, education, research writing, and public-facing instruction.

## Admission
- Standard university admission policy
- Placement: writing diagnostic recommended

## Completion Requirements
- 120 credits total
- Minimum grade standard per university policy
- Completion of capstone (BTS-499) with passing rubric score
