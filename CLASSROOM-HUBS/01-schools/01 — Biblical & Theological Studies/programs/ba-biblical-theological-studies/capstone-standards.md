# Capstone Standards — BTS-499

## Capstone Artifacts (Portfolio)
1) Major Passage Exegesis (method-based)
2) Theological Synthesis Essay (theme across canon)
3) Research Paper (8–12 pages, cited)
4) Teaching Artifact (lesson plan + short lecture or script)
5) Reflection: ethics, integrity, and responsible claims

## Evaluation
Capstone is assessed with a standardized rubric and must demonstrate:
- method clarity
- contextual control
- evidence-based reasoning
- citation and integrity
- responsible public communication
