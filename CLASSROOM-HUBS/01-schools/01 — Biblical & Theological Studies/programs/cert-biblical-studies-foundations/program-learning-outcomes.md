# Program Learning Outcomes (PLOs) — Biblical Studies Foundations (Certificate)

PLO-1: Explain how SydTek courses function (content + GitHub materials + assessments) and apply basic academic integrity.
PLO-2: Apply the Mirror → Water → Fire method to interpret a biblical passage with explicit reasoning.
PLO-3: Identify common interpretive errors and demonstrate responsible cross-referencing and contextual reading.
PLO-4: Summarize major Torah covenant themes and connect them to later biblical theology carefully (without forced claims).
PLO-5: Produce short, structured theological writing artifacts (portfolio) that demonstrate clarity, evidence-mindedness, and ethical application.
