# School of Skilled Trades & Intelligent Infrastructure

## Purpose
Provides workforce-ready training for electricians and infrastructure technicians needed to build and maintain power, automation, and data-center systems in the AI era.

## Scope
- electrical fundamentals, commercial wiring, safety codes, smart infrastructure, data-center power basics, automation maintenance
- Credentials: certificates, diplomas, apprenticeship-aligned pathways
- Delivery: hands-on + hybrid theory instruction

## Distinctives
- Safety + code compliance emphasis (documentation and demonstrations)
- Job-ready competencies with performance-based testing
- Pathways into high-demand roles supporting AI infrastructure
