# q001 — Introduction: How This Campus Works (Orientation)

## Quest Type
Orientation (Badge-eligible)

## Method Mode
MIRROR → WATER → FIRE (lightweight)

## Estimated Time
5–10 minutes

## Objective
By the end, the learner can:
- Navigate the campus zones
- Explain what “proof artifacts” are
- Explain Mirror/Water/Fire at a basic level
- Identify required disclosures (education-only, not financial advice)

## Required Reading / Signs
- Spawn Hub “Disclosures” board
- Spawn Hub “Campus Map” board

## Steps

### Step 1 — MIRROR: Observe the System
1. Go to the **Campus Map Board**.
2. Identify the 7 zones:
   - Spawn Hub
   - SydTek Library
   - Concordance Archives
   - Mirror → Water → Fire Hall
   - Exhibit Walkthrough
   - Lecture Theater
   - Creator Studio
3. Answer:
   - What zone teaches anchors?
   - What zone teaches cross-links?
   - What zone teaches application?

### Step 2 — WATER: Connect the Flow
4. Speak to the **Registrar NPC**.
5. Ask: “What is a proof artifact?”
6. Record the flow in 1–2 sentences:
   - Orientation → Anchor → Cross-link → Method → Capstone → Credential

### Step 3 — FIRE: Apply a Safe Next Step
7. Choose ONE next action:
   - Start q002 in the Library, OR
   - Take the Exhibit Walkthrough first
8. State why you chose it in one sentence.

## Proof Artifact (Submit)
Submit a short response (150–250 words) or a screenshot + caption that includes:

1) **Mirror (2–3 bullets):**  
- What you observed about zones and navigation

2) **Water (2–3 bullets):**  
- How the campus learning loop works

3) **Fire (1 bullet):**  
- Your next action and why

4) **Disclosures acknowledgement (1 line):**  
- “I understand this is education-only and not financial advice, and credentials are proof-based.”
