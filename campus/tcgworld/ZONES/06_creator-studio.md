# Zone — Creator Studio (Build + Submit)

## Purpose
Enable learners to contribute safely:
- quests
- NPC scripts
- placards
- lesson plans
with governance gates and rubrics.

## What exists here (minimum)
- Submission booth (“Open a PR / Issue” instructions)
- Templates board (quest template, NPC template, rubric template)
- Rubric board (capstone rubric + quality gates)
- Creator Studio Mentor NPC placement
- q004 start board

## Required Sign Text (short)
Build responsibly. Cite anchors. Disclose AI use.  
No fabricated claims. No guarantees. No coercion.

## Quests Routed
- q004_capstone

## Completion Signal
Capstone PR merged or memo approved.
