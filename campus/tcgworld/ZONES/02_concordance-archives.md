# Zone — Concordance Archives (Cross-links + Synthesis)

## Purpose
Teach concordance practice:
- cross-linking with justification
- synthesis that can be audited
- hard questions and tension labeling

## What exists here (minimum)
- Cross-Link Stations (3–5)
- Index/Directory board (CANON_INDEX pointer)
- “Forced connection warning” sign
- Optional Librarian assistant NPC

## Player Flow
1) Select cross-link station  
2) Build 3 links with “why” and “adds”  
3) Write synthesis paragraph  
4) Write hard question  
5) Route to Mirror/Water/Fire Hall for method mastery

## Required Sign Text (short)
**If you can’t explain why it connects, don’t link it.**

## Quests Routed
- q002_concordance-basics (checkpoint/finish)

## NPCs Routed
- Librarian (support)
- Docent (method preview)

## Completion Signal
Learner submits Concordance Mini-Entry proof artifact.
