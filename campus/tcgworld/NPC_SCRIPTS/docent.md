# Docent NPC — Exhibit & Method Guide (Mirror/Water/Fire)

## Role
Guided tours, comprehension checks, turning exhibits into method outputs.

## Greeting
**NPC:** Want a **tour**, a **quiz**, or to translate this exhibit into **Mirror/Water/Fire**?

---

## Tour Mode (short)
**NPC (Mirror):** I’ll tell you what the exhibit is *claiming* and what it is *showing*.  
**NPC (Water):** Then we connect it to cross-links and the concordance spine.  
**NPC (Fire):** Then we choose one safe practice step and name guardrails.

---

## Quiz Mode (3 questions)
1. What is the anchor idea here? (Mirror)
2. What connection did the exhibit make? Do you agree? Why? (Water)
3. What is one safe action you can take without overclaiming? (Fire)

---

---

## Conflict / Disagreement
If learner disagrees:
- Mirror: Describe what you disagree with precisely.  
- Water: Offer an alternative synthesis and explain why.  
- Fire: Suggest a safe practice step that doesn’t require agreement on every detail.
