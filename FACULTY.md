# SydTek Faculty and Assessor Model

SydTek University uses the term faculty broadly to describe the human-guided instructional and verification layer within the educational ecosystem.

Until a larger institutional structure is formalized, faculty functions may be carried by:

• founder-instructors  
• pathway designers  
• assessors  
• reviewers  
• mentors  
• guest scholars  

This document defines those roles in a disciplined way.

---

## Core Principle

A structured learning institution must distinguish between:

• content creator  
• pathway designer  
• assessor  
• academic reviewer  
• institutional authority  

One person may hold more than one role, but the roles themselves should remain conceptually distinct.

---

## Faculty Roles

### Founder-Instructor

Primary function:
Defines institutional vision, key pathways, and flagship teaching content.

Typical responsibilities:
• shapes academic direction  
• develops core doctrine-linked programs  
• records flagship playlists  
• approves pathway architecture  

---

### Pathway Designer

Primary function:
Builds course and pathway structures.

Typical responsibilities:
• sequences playlists  
• aligns books to programs  
• defines writing requirements  
• creates assessment flow  
• drafts metadata and registry logic  

---

### Assessor

Primary function:
Reviews learner outputs and verifies completion.

Typical responsibilities:
• checks reflections  
• evaluates projects  
• verifies pathway requirements  
• approves completion records  
• provides written feedback where applicable  

---

### Academic Reviewer

Primary function:
Reviews program consistency and public claims.

Typical responsibilities:
• checks pathway integrity  
• reviews catalog language  
• verifies policy alignment  
• helps prevent overclaiming  
• supports institutional quality control  

---

### Mentor

Primary function:
Supports learner progress informally or semi-formally.

Typical responsibilities:
• encourages completion  
• explains pathway flow  
• supports study discipline  
• helps learners interpret expectations  

---

## NPC Faculty Layer

NPC professors in TCG.world function as symbolic and navigational faculty, not as final institutional authorities.

They may:

• introduce modules  
• explain themes  
• assign quests  
• direct learners to content  
• reinforce progression logic  

They should not be treated as the final verification layer unless backed by human or system-based record verification.

---

## Minimum Faculty Standard for Program Activation

A program should not be considered operational until it has at least:

• one pathway designer  
• one assessor or reviewer role defined  
• one named oversight authority  

Even if the same person serves all three roles initially, the repo should document the distinctions.

---

## Faculty Metadata Recommendation

Each faculty or assessor entry should eventually include:

• name  
• role  
• domain  
• pathway responsibility  
• verification authority level  
• active status  

This can later be represented in JSON or YAML for AI readability.

---

## Example Minimal Faculty Structure

• Founder-Instructor: directs mission and flagship content  
• Program Designer: structures pathway sequence  
• Assessor: verifies outputs and completion  
• Reviewer: checks claims and consistency  

---

## Summary

The faculty model gives SydTek a real instructional backbone.

Without faculty logic, the system is content.
With faculty logic, the system becomes education.
