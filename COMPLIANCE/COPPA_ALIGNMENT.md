## 1. Scope

This document outlines compliance alignment
with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Applies to users under 13 years of age.

---

## 2. Parental Consent Requirement

No minor under 13 may:

- Create account
- Submit coursework
- Engage with AI systems

without verifiable parental consent.

Consent must be:
- Documented
- Revocable
- Logged

---

## 3. Data Collection Limits

For minors:

- No behavioral tracking
- No voiceprint storage
- No biometric capture
- No targeted personalization

Only essential educational data may be collected.

---

## 4. AI Interaction Safeguards

NPCs and AI agents interacting with minors must:

- Be clearly labeled as AI
- Avoid authority simulation
- Avoid emotional manipulation
- Avoid persuasive nudging

---

## 5. Parental Rights

Parents may:

- Review collected data
- Request deletion
- Revoke consent

Requests must be fulfilled within 30 days.

---

## 6. Advertising Restrictions

No advertising, token promotion, or financial
solicitation is permitted in minor environments.

---

## 7. Enforcement & Reporting

Violations trigger:

- Immediate suspension
- Governance investigation
- Public transparency summary

---

Annual review required.
