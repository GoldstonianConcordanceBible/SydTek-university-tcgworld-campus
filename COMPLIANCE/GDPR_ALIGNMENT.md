# GDPR Alignment Statement
SydTek University – AI & Metaverse Systems

## 1. Scope

This document describes SydTek University’s alignment
with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
for users located within the European Union.

---

## 2. Lawful Basis for Processing

Data is processed under:

- Explicit consent
- Educational contract necessity
- Legitimate academic interest

Consent must be:
- Freely given
- Specific
- Informed
- Revocable

---

## 3. Data Subject Rights

Users may:

- Access their data
- Correct inaccuracies
- Request deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
- Restrict processing
- Request portability
- Object to automated decision-making

AI systems must not deny human review upon request.

---

## 4. Data Minimization

Only data necessary for:

- Course delivery
- Credential verification
- Academic integrity

is collected.

No behavioral profiling for commercial purposes.

---

## 5. Automated Decision-Making

If AI significantly impacts a student:

- The system must disclose automation use
- Provide explanation
- Allow human review

---

## 6. Data Storage & Security

- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Role-based access control
- Audit logging

Blockchain entries must not contain personal data.

---

## 7. Data Retention

Retention periods:

- Coursework: 7 years
- Credential records: indefinite (hash only)
- Interaction logs: 12 months unless required longer

---

## 8. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

SydTek governance must designate an AI/Data Oversight Lead.

---

Reviewed annually under Governance Review Cadence.
