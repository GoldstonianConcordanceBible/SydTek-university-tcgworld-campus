# Course 03 — The Gospels Overview: Kingdom, Messiah, Cross, Resurrection

## Course Description
An overview of Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John with attention to each Gospel’s purpose, audience, key themes, and how they present Jesus. Students learn to read each Gospel on its own terms before harmonizing.

## Recommended Time
10–15 hours total

## Modules
1. What a Gospel is (genre + purpose)
2. Matthew: kingdom and fulfillment
3. Mark: suffering servant and discipleship
4. Luke: salvation for all and ethical reversal
5. John: Word made flesh and belief
6. Passion week: cross, resurrection, and meaning

## Final Requirement
Write a “Four-Fold Portrait” (800–1200 words):
- 1 paragraph per Gospel (purpose + key emphasis)
- a short conclusion: what changes when you respect distinct voices
