# Badge Taxonomy — GCB Campus (TCG.world)

## Naming Convention

`gcb-tcg-badge-<badge_code>-<serial>`

Examples:
- `gcb-tcg-badge-orientation-000001`
- `gcb-tcg-badge-concordance-000014`

---

## Badge List

### 1) Orientation Badge
**Code:** `orientation`  
**Earned by:** q001_introduction  
**Competencies:**
- Understand zone navigation
- Understand proof + attestation workflow
- Understand conduct/disclosure basics

---

### 2) Concordance Navigator Badge
**Code:** `concordance`  
**Earned by:** q002_concordance-basics  
**Competencies:**
- Cite anchor correctly
- Produce 3 valid cross-links
- Identify one “hard question” honestly

---

### 3) Mirror/Water/Fire Method Badge
**Code:** `mwf`  
**Earned by:** q003_mirror-water-fire  
**Competencies:**
- Mirror: accurate observation and definitions
- Water: coherent synthesis across links
- Fire: practical application with ethical guardrails

---

### 4) Capstone Badge
**Code:** `capstone`  
**Earned by:** q004_capstone  
**Competencies:**
- Create a publishable artifact OR pass a capstone memo
- Demonstrate attribution and integrity
- Meet quality gate requirements

---

## Certificate Bundle

### GCB Campus Certificate (TCG.world)
**Code:** `certificate`  
**Requires:** orientation + concordance + mwf + capstone (same program version window)
