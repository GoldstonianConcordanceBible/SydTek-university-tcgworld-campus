# Assessments (GCB Campus)

This folder contains assessment rubrics and templates used across quests, exhibits, and capstones.

## Principles
- Evidence first (Mirror)
- Coherent synthesis (Water)
- Safe practice with accountability (Fire)
- Proof artifacts must be auditable and versioned

## Contents (recommended)
- `rubric_orientation.md`
- `rubric_concordance.md`
- `rubric_mwf.md`
- `rubric_capstone.md`
- `rubric_capstone_phd_level.md` (optional)

## Standard Scoring
Most quests use:
- Total: 10 points
- Pass: 8 points

Capstone can use:
- 10-point baseline OR 100-point PhD rubric for advanced cohorts
