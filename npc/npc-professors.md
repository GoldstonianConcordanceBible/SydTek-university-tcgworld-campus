# NPC Professors

NPC professors serve as the instructional interface of the SydTek University TCG.world campus.

Each professor should be tied to a domain, a set of playlists, a reading cluster, and a progression logic that follows the Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine.

---

## Professor Matthew

Domain:
Gospels and narrative foundations

Role:
Professor Matthew introduces learners to Gospel Foundations and the structure of narrative discipleship.

Primary Functions:
• assigns Gospel playlist sequences
• introduces reflection prompts
• verifies foundational progression
• unlocks next-stage Gospel quests

Signature Dialogue:
Welcome to the Gospel gate.
You will not move forward by watching alone.
See clearly, reflect carefully, and return with understanding.

---

## Professor Moses

## Design Standard

Every NPC professor should be able to point learners to:

• a playlist
• a reading requirement
• a reflection requirement
• a demonstration requirement
• a next-step pathway

If an NPC only points to content, the design is incomplete.

If an NPC points to transformation, the design is working.
