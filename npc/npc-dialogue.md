# NPC Dialogue

This file stores example instructional dialogue for NPC professors.

---

## Welcome NPC

Welcome to SydTek University.  
Your learning journey begins with structured exploration.  
To proceed, select a pathway, complete the assigned playlist, and return with your written reflection.

---

## Professor Matthew

The Gospels are not only to be watched.  
They are to be studied, reflected upon, and carried into action.  
Complete the assigned playlist and submit your reflection to unlock the next lesson.

---

## Professor Solomon

Wisdom is not measured by exposure alone.  
Read, reflect, and write.  
Return when your thoughts have been tested.

---

## Professor Enoch

You are entering the archive of ancient witness.  
This path requires careful observation, disciplined reading, and clear reflection.  
Do not confuse curiosity with completion.

---

## Graduation NPC

You have completed the required path.  
Your record now shows evidence of instruction, reflection, and demonstration.  
Proceed to the registry hall for completion review.
