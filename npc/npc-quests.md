# NPC Quests

NPC quests convert curriculum into interactive progression.

Each quest should have:

• quest title  
• NPC guide  
• objective  
• linked playlist  
• linked reading  
• required output  
• completion trigger  

---

## Example Quests

### Quest: Enter the Gospel Gate
NPC Guide: Professor Matthew  
Objective: Complete the Gospel Foundations playlist  
Reading: GCB Companion Volume I  
Output: 500-word reflection  
Completion Trigger: Reflection verified

### Quest: The Watchers Archive
NPC Guide: Professor Enoch  
Objective: Complete the Enoch orientation playlist  
Reading: Canon Orientation Guide  
Output: concept map  
Completion Trigger: Map uploaded

### Quest: Fire of Demonstration
NPC Guide: Professor Paul  
Objective: Synthesize doctrine across three playlists  
Reading: Doctrine Companion  
Output: comparative essay  
Completion Trigger: Essay approved

---

## Design Rule

Every quest should reinforce the sequence:

Mirror → Water → Fire

Quest design should never stop at viewing content alone.
