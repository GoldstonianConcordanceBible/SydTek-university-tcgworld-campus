# Reason Codes — Corrections, Revisions, Revocations (MWF Governance)

These codes standardize why an action was taken.

---

# 1) Correction Codes (content fixes)
- **CORR_ANCHOR_MISQUOTE** — anchor misquoted or misrepresented
- **CORR_MISSING_BOUNDARY** — boundaries absent causing overreach
- **CORR_FORCED_CROSSLINK** — cross-link lacked justification
- **CORR_TENSION_FLATTENED** — tension ignored or smoothed improperly
- **CORR_GUARDRAIL_MISSING** — Fire application lacked guardrails
- **CORR_DISCLOSURE_MISSING** — required disclosure absent

---

# 2) Revision Codes (structured improvements)
- **REV_RUBRIC_CLARITY** — rubric rewritten for clarity
- **REV_TEMPLATE_UPDATE** — template update (non-breaking)
- **REV_ZONE_FLOW** — campus flow improvement
- **REV_AGENT_TRAINING** — training curriculum refinement
- **REV_TEST_SUITE_EXPAND** — expanded tests / improved coverage

---

# 3) Revocation Codes (credential invalidation)
- **REVOKE_FABRICATION** — fabricated anchors/citations/policies
- **REVOKE_PRIVACY** — private data disclosed or requested improperly
- **REVOKE_ILLEGAL_ASSIST** — illegal guidance or bypass assistance
- **REVOKE_COERCION** — manipulation, shame, fear leverage, dominance framing
- **REVOKE_CREDENTIAL_GAMING** — cheating, bypass attempts, falsified evidence
- **REVOKE_VERSION_MISMATCH** — issuance on wrong program_version
- **REVOKE_REVIEW_FRAUD** — forged signatures or falsified review records

---

# 4) Drift Event Codes (attribution)
- **DRIFT_HUMAN_FORCED** — operator pressure drove attempted drift
- **DRIFT_AGENT_INTERNAL** — drift occurred without operator pressure
- **DRIFT_CONTENT** — drift caused by outdated/contradictory source content
- **DRIFT_PROCESS** — drift due to missing governance gates/tests

---

# 5) Severity Levels
- LOW
- MEDIUM
- HIGH
- CRITICAL

CRITICAL requires immediate suspension pending review.
