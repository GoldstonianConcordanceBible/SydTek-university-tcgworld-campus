# Academic Integrity Policy

SydTek University and SydTek Scholars require honest participation in all learning pathways.

Learners are expected to submit their own work unless collaboration is expressly permitted.

---

## Violations May Include

• submitting writing created by another person as original work  
• fabricating watch-hour completion  
• falsifying reading completion  
• copying projects without attribution  
• misrepresenting assessment results  

---

## AI Use

AI tools may be used only within the rules established for a given course or pathway.

If AI assistance is permitted, the learner should disclose where and how it was used.

AI should support understanding, not replace learning.

---

## Standard

The purpose of the SydTek model is transformation through study, reflection, and demonstration.

Any act that destroys the integrity of that process undermines the educational mission.
