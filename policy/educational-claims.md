# Educational Claims Policy

SydTek University and SydTek Scholars must communicate clearly and accurately about their educational status.

---

## Allowed Public Claims

Examples of acceptable language include:

• structured learning framework  
• certificate pathway  
• independent study pathway  
• homeschool support model  
• accreditation-ready framework  
• curriculum infrastructure  

---

## Restricted Claims

The institution should not claim:

• accredited degree granting authority  
• recognized accreditation status  
• transferability of credits to third-party institutions  
• state approval where none exists  

unless those claims are formally and verifiably true.

---

## Why This Matters

Clear public language protects the integrity of the institution and prevents confusion.

It also helps distinguish serious educational architecture from inflated claims.
