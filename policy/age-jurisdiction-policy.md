# Age and Jurisdiction Policy

SydTek learning pathways are implemented differently depending on the learner's age and governing jurisdiction.

---

## Minors

For learners below the legal age of educational self-direction in their jurisdiction, pathway selection and oversight should be handled by a parent, guardian, or lawful educational supervisor.

This may include selection of:

• playlists  
• books  
• pacing  
• assessments  
• reporting methods  

---

## Adults

For learners at or above the relevant legal age in their jurisdiction, pathway selection may be self-directed unless another institutional rule applies.

---

## Legal Responsibility

SydTek provides educational structure and documentation models.

The learner, parent, or responsible party remains responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable law, homeschool rules, and reporting requirements in the relevant jurisdiction.

---

## Institutional Position

SydTek does not claim that a single universal implementation satisfies all jurisdictions.

The framework is adaptable and should be localized where needed.
