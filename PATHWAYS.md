# SydTek Pathways

Pathways are the operational routes learners follow through SydTek Scholars and SydTek University.

A program defines the destination.  
A pathway defines the journey.

---

## Pathway Design Logic

Every pathway should answer five questions:

1. What does the learner study?  
2. In what sequence?  
3. What evidence is required?  
4. How is completion verified?  
5. What becomes available next?  

If those questions are not answered, the pathway is incomplete.

---

## Pathway Components

A complete pathway should include:

• pathway title  
• learner type  
• entry requirement  
• playlist sequence  
• reading sequence  
• reflection sequence  
• project or assessment requirement  
• completion verification  
• next-step options  

---

## Example Pathway Types

### Guided Homeschool Pathway
Used primarily in SydTek Scholars.

Features:
• parent-selected playlists  
• age-appropriate reading  
• narration or short writing  
• portfolio artifacts  
• parent verification  

---

### Foundational Certificate Pathway
Used primarily for first-level adult and advanced learners.

Features:
• clear playlist sequence  
• one to three required books  
• defined reflection count  
• final quiz or essay  
• registry-based completion  

---

### Integrative Doctrine Pathway
Used for GCB-specific interpretive study.

Features:
• doctrine-linked playlists  
• reflection prompts tied to Mirror → Water → Fire  
• synthesis writing  
• concept mapping  
• demonstrative output  

---

### Research Pathway
Used for advanced learners or fellows.

Features:
• curated advanced reading  
• independent playlist clusters  
• annotated writing  
• thesis or research artifact  
• assessor review

## Pathway Levels

Pathways may be tagged by level.

### Foundational
Introduces major concepts and basic study habits.

### Intermediate
Requires sustained reading, more writing, and thematic comparison.

### Advanced
Requires synthesis, independent organization, and significant demonstration.

### Research
Requires thesis-style output, public scholarship, or advanced annotation.

---

## Mirror → Water → Fire Pathway Standard

Every serious pathway should include:

### Mirror
• playlists  
• primary texts  
• direct encounter with material  

### Water
• reflections  
• annotation  
• journaling  
• discussion  
• formative checks  

### Fire
• essays  
• projects  
• presentations  
• teaching artifacts  
• thesis work  

A pathway without Fire is incomplete.

---

## NPC Integration

In the TCG.world campus, pathways may be surfaced through:

• NPC assignment  
• quest chains  
• unlockable progress gates  
• symbolic completion ceremonies  

The NPC layer should never replace the formal record.  
It should amplify the learner’s journey through it.

## Summary

Pathways are the scaffolding that turn SydTek from a media ecosystem into an educational system.
