# YouTube Discussion Policy

SydTek University courses incorporate YouTube-based discussion to facilitate open theological dialogue.

Students must:

1. Watch the assigned lecture video.
2. Post a thoughtful comment engaging the lecture content.
3. Respond to at least two other participants.
4. Submit the comment link to the course submission system.

Comments must demonstrate:

• Engagement with lecture material
• Biblical or theological reasoning
• Respectful interaction
• Academic tone

Inappropriate comments may result in removal and resubmission requirements.
