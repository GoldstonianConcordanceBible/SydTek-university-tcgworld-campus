# SydTek University
## YouTube Discussion System

This repository documents the official **SydTek University Public Discussion System**, which integrates YouTube lecture engagement into accredited academic coursework.

The system is designed to support:

• Open theological discourse  
• Global collaboration  
• Decentralized scholarship (DeSci)  
• Verifiable student interaction  

This model aligns with the **Goldstonian Concordance Bible educational framework** and the SydTek University mission of open-access theological education.

---

# Pedagogical Model

Traditional model:

Lecture → LMS forum → Instructor review

SydTek model:

Lecture (YouTube)  
→ Global discussion (YouTube comments)  
→ Student link submission  
→ Faculty grading

Students participate in public theological discussions under course lecture videos.

Their contributions are then **archived and assessed through the SydTek submission system**.

---

# Why This Model

Benefits include:

• Public theological engagement  
• Scalable discussion environment  
• Global peer interaction  
• Algorithmic discovery of lectures  
• Integration with open scholarship ecosystems

---

# Accreditation Compliance

To meet accreditation standards, SydTek University maintains:

• Documented participation  
• Instructor feedback  
• Graded discussion rubrics  
• Submission verification  
• Academic integrity policies  

All student participation is recorded through submitted discussion links and archived in course records.

---

# Workflow Overview

Step 1  
Student watches lecture.

Step 2  
Student posts a comment engaging the lecture.

Step 3  
Student replies to two peers.

Step 4  
Student submits comment link.

Step 5  
Instructor reviews and grades submission.
